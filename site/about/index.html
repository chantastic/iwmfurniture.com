Who we are

With over 55 years of combined experience, Innovative World Market, Inc. and factories have been a leader in the furniture industry, specializing in high quality case good furniture for both home and contract markets. In home, hospitality, and custom design, Innovative World Market excels in matching your need with great craftsmanship and ingenuity.

Innovative World Market, Inc. has factories throughout Asia, each with a distinct personality. This unique structure affords us the flexibility to supply for a wide variety of markets, at the the highest possible quality. We believe that high quality products can only be achieved, in all classes, by isolating products to factories with complimentary strengths.

At Innovative World Market, Inc. we take pride in offering each client superior service with market leading experience. Our experience allows us to thrive where others fall short, balancing design and functionality. Demonstrating relentless attention to detail our staff goes the extra mile to guarantee the beauty and longevity of your products. It is our joy to be your manufacturer direct source.
